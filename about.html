About us

Championing online privacy and security since 2013

Everyone has the right to privacy, even online. That’s why we created MEGA – to help you keep the information you store and share online private and secure.

Our mission

To provide a secure and private space for everyone to store, share, and exchange information through powerful and innovative products that allow users to decide who can access their information.

Our vision

To create a world where everyone’s information is secure and private by default.

Our promise

With MEGA, you control your data.

We protect your data and respect your privacy

We use the most advanced security and privacy measures and tools to protect your information and put you in control of your data.

    • MEGA is born

    After three months of coding by Mathias Ortmann and Bram van der Kolk, MEGA is released with immediate global publicity. One million users register in the first three days.

    One billion files uploaded

    In just over 12 months, MEGA users have stored more than one billion files in their secure accounts.

    MEGA Chat beta released

    MEGA expands its product offerings with MEGA Chat, our secure, end-to-end encrypted communications platform with easy file sharing built in.

    MEGA Chat: secure text, voice and video chat

    MEGA Chat provides secure communications with end-to-end encryption for web browsers and mobile apps (Android and iOS).

    MEGA Command Line released

    MEGA releases a powerful scriptable interactive command shell for advanced users and sysadmins.

    100 million registered MEGA users

    Rapid business growth sees MEGA reach 100 million registered users, who together have uploaded more than 40 billion files.

    MEGA for Business released

    We release our high-performance enterprise storage solution, MEGA for Business, with multi-user support and large-capacity storage options.

    MEGA reaches 200 million users

    October 2020 saw MEGA hit the 200 million registered users milestone.

    Hypergrowth

    MEGA continues to grow rapidly and now employs over 200 staff worldwide.

    Cookie settings

    Essential cookies

    Essential for providing you important functionality and secure access to our services. For this reason, they do not require consent.

    Preferences cookies

    Persistently remember your preferences such as language and theme.

    Analytics cookies

    Help us to understand how you use our services, and provide us data that we can use to make improvements and provide a personalised experience. Not accepting these cookies means we will have less data available to us to help design improvements.

    Marketing cookies

    Help us measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns and deliver targeted advertisements of our products and services by collecting data about your device and behaviour on our website and tracking you across devices and marketing channels. Not accepting these cookies means your information won’t be tracked when you visit our website and you are much less likely to view relevant offers about our products and services.
