CHAT AND MEETINGS

Every message and call is 100% private

Speak freely on MEGA knowing that your messages, one-on-one calls, and meetings are all private and secure. That’s because all conversations are encrypted, which means we can’t read your messages or listen to your calls.

Start chatting

Send and share sensitive information

Instead of texting or emailing, use MEGA to send sensitive or important information. More about security.

Speak freely

We use zero-knowledge encryption, which means only the intended recipients or invited participants can read your messages or listen to your calls. No one else can, not even MEGA.

Learn more

Make your group chats extra secure

Enable encryption key rotation to effectively turn your group chat into a closed chat. This stops anyone from sharing a link to the chat, prevents new participants from seeing old messages and stops participants who’ve left the chat from seeing new messages.

Share data and still have control

Upload and share any file from your device to chat, or easily share your MEGA files over chat and still have control. You can share either as a link (read-only access) or by sharing a folder (read-only, read and write, or full access).

More about sharing

Chat and meetings for everyone

Invite anyone to join a meeting or group chat

It’s easy to catch up with groups of friends and colleagues on MEGA—even if they don’t have a MEGA account. You can invite anyone to join your group chat or meeting and guests just need to click on the link that’s been shared with them.

No downloads or sign-ups needed

You don’t need to download anything or sign up to MEGA to join a meeting or group chat from your computer. You don’t even need a phone number. You just need a link from the meeting host and you’re ready to go.

If you want to join a group chat or meeting from your mobile, you won’t need to sign up to MEGA, but you will need to download one of our mobile apps.

Catch up on the go from any device and from anywhere

With MEGA, you can take the cloud with you to make sure you don’t miss an important meeting or conversation. You can join a meeting or group chat from your computer or phone; just go to the chat or meetings section or, if you’re a non-MEGA user, open the link that’s been shared with you using a browser or our mobile apps.

Collaborate easily and seamlessly

Teammates, friends, and customers – no matter who your audience is – can all collaborate easily and securely with MEGA Chat.

Chat

Send messages, files, GIFs, and emojis to one person or a group. Anyone with the link can join a group chat, including people without MEGA accounts.

Calls and meetings

Make audio and video calls on MEGA. You can also schedule or start an instant meeting and invite over 100 people, including people who don’t have a MEGA account. Just send them a calendar invite or a link. Invitees can join as guests by clicking on the link.

Add and share contacts

Easily add new contacts to MEGA – just send them an invite. You can also send your MEGA contacts to others in a chat or meeting.

Screen share

Easily share your presentations, documents or screen with friends and colleagues in a MEGA meeting.

Communicate seamlessly from any device

Chat, call, and have meetings across multiple devices – computers, tablets, and phones.

Share files in a chat or meeting

Upload and share any file from your device to a chat or meeting.

Share your MEGA files and folders

Share your MEGA files over chat – either as a link (read-only access) or by sharing a folder (read-only, read-and-write, or full access). More about sharing.

Format your messages

You can use markup (surround text with special characters) to format your messages in chat – such as bold, italic, block quotes.

More features on their way

Waiting room for meetings

Meeting hosts can turn on this feature to prevent all participants from joining the call straight away, and instead, place them into a virtual waiting room. The host can then decide who joins the meeting and when.

Mentions in a chat

If you want to alert someone, or multiple people, in a meeting or a group chat, you can simply add an @ sign in front of a user name, and they’ll be notified. Only MEGA users can be mentioned.

Reply to a message with context

You can use “Reply to” in a chat or meeting to include the reference to the message you’re responding to. This means your reply will have the relevant context.
