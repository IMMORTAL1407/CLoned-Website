Secure and generous storage solution for your business

Bring your team together online, boost and maintain productivity, and transform how your team collaborates with MEGA for Business. Whether you’re a freelancer or a startup founder or work for a corporate, MEGA has all you need to grow your business, in one place.

Get MEGA for Business

Powerful cloud storage

Get flexible storage

With MEGA for Business, there’s no limit on how much storage or transfer you can use.

Pay for what you use

You’re charged monthly for your storage and transfer usage. So, you can use as little as 3 TB of storage or transfer in one month and as much as 16 TB, or even more in another month. You don’t need to pay extra for what you might use later or never use. Learn more about Business account pricing and our competitive prices

Keep your business files at your fingertips

Access your business files, client documents, and employee information wherever and whenever you need through a web browser or via our mobile apps.

Upload large files

With MEGA’s unlimited file size, no file is ever too big. To make your data upload even faster, use the MEGA Desktop App.

Securely do business online

Run your business securely and protect your clients’ information

Zero-knowledge encryption

With our zero-knowledge encryption, no one, not even us, can access or see your data. Several keys to protect your data are created when you sign up for MEGA, and you’re the only one who holds these keys and can use them to decrypt and see your information.

Share securely

If you want to share files or folders with your clients, send them password-protected links with expiry dates ensuring your clients’ sensitive information stays confidential. You can also delete these links at any time and remove access to the files and folders.

Receive files reliably

Use our “file requests” and invite your clients to share files and information privately and securely with you. They don’t need to sign up for MEGA.

Prevent data loss

Sync your computer with MEGA

Sync selected folders on your computer with MEGA to ensure you have a digital safety net in case of accidents, cyber attacks, or system outages. Syncing replicates updates to folders on your device with MEGA and vice versa.

Back up your data to MEGA

Backup is a one-way sync and automatically copies any files, folders, and changes to these files and folders from your computer to MEGA. This will ensure that the most up-to-date version of your important business documents is saved on MEGA and will never be lost.

FAQ

How does MEGA for Business help you grow your business?

MEGA’s Business account helps you bring your team together online and facilitates decision-making. Time is spent on completing tasks and ticking off projects rather than on trying to find better ways to collaborate, manage and share content, or meet.

How can you increase productivity in the workplace with MEGA for Business?

With MEGA’s Business account, your team can centralise work in one place, and share and collaborate with ease using MEGA’s chat, audio or video calls, and screen sharing. You’re also able to streamline workflows when your team is working together on a project with strict deadlines.

How does MEGA encourage and improve team collaboration?

MEGA brings your information and people together in one workspace and makes collaboration effortless and easy. Your team is only a click or two away from sharing important files, like meeting agendas or project timelines, with both their colleagues and clients. They can also chat or use our audio or video calling to safely and securely discuss important and sensitive work matters with their teammates.
